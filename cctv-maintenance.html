CCTV Maintenance & Support

Helping Your Security System Perform Reliably

Regular maintenance helps extend the lifespan of CCTV equipment and reduces the likelihood of unexpected system issues. Over time, cameras can collect dust, cables may become loose, storage devices can reach capacity, and software may require updates. Periodic inspections help identify these issues before they affect the overall performance of the surveillance system.

At Marutinandan CCTV Security, we provide maintenance and repair services for residential, commercial, and industrial CCTV systems. Our technicians examine camera performance, recording devices, power connections, network settings, and remote viewing functionality to help keep your surveillance system operating efficiently.

  • Complete CCTV system inspection
  • Camera cleaning and image quality checks
  • DVR and NVR diagnostics
  • Hard drive testing and replacement
  • Network and remote viewing configuration
  • Firmware and software updates
  • Cable inspection and connection testing
  • Support for existing CCTV installations

Our CCTV Maintenance Services

Routine System Inspection

Regular inspections help identify worn components, loose connections, and other potential issues before they affect camera performance. We check the overall condition of your surveillance system to support reliable operation.

Camera Cleaning & Performance Checks

Dust, moisture, and environmental exposure can reduce image clarity over time. We clean camera lenses, verify image quality, and inspect recording performance to help maintain consistent surveillance.

DVR & NVR Diagnostics

Our technicians examine recording devices, storage health, network connectivity, and system settings. If required, we replace faulty hard drives, update firmware, and optimise the system for dependable daily use.
